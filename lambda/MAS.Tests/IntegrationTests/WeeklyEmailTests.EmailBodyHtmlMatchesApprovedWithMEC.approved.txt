                                        <a class="article-link" href="https://jamanetwork.com/journals/jama/fullarticle/2759414">
                                            Effect of Vitamin C, Hydrocortisone, and Thiamine vs Hydrocortisone Alone on Time Alive and Free of Vasopressor Support Among Patients With Septic Shock
                                        </a>
                                </h3>

                                        <a class="article-link" href="https://www.thelancet.com/journals/lanpsy/article/PIIS2215-0366(18)30471-1/fulltext">
                                            Opioid prescribing trends and geographical variation in England
                                        </a>
                                </h3>
                                
                                <p class="article-description">
                                    There were substantial increase in opioid (OP) prescribing in NHS between 1998 and 2016; OP prescriptions increased by 34% (from 568/1000 patients to 761/1000). After correcting for total oral morphine equivalency, increase was 127% (from 190,000 to 431,000mg/1000 population).
                                </p>
