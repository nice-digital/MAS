Guidance to clinicians: The process for prescribing Cannabis-based products for medicinal use

NHS England have produced guidance to support clinicians for the process of prescribing cannabis-based products for medicinal use. It is intended as a document to help patients access cannabis-based products for medicinal use where clinically appropriate.
